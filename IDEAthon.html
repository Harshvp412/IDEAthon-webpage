About Us

The event is organized by Entrepreneurship Cell of IIT Madras which is a Non-Profit Organisation running to help budding entrepreneurs. Entrepreneurship Cell at IIT Madras believes that entrepreneurship is not just about starting companies, but a pathway towards India’s socio-economic development.

Getting started with Entrepreneurship

We are sure that many of our audience in the campus is struggling to get started with entrepreneurship even if they are interested in Entrepreneurship as well as the audience who have their own Ideas but just can’t find a point to start with them , or even if they started they backed out in the middle due to the lack of basic knowledge and entrepreneurial approach .
So, Let’s start before you are ready with Ideathon , an exclusive event of E-Cell for exclusive IIT Madras community.
Learning never dies, even if it's quarantine , so tighten your seat belts and get started with an awesome journey ahead.
